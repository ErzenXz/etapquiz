About eTapQuiz

eTapQuiz is a quiz app inspired by Kahoot. It allows users to create and participate in interactive quizzes. With a user-friendly interface and engaging features, eTapQuiz makes learning fun and interactive.

Whether you are a student looking to test your knowledge or a teacher wanting to create educational quizzes, eTapQuiz has got you covered. Create your own quizzes or explore a wide range of quizzes created by other users.

eTapQuiz offers a variety of question types, including multiple choice, true/false, and open-ended questions. It also provides real-time feedback and a leaderboard to track your progress and compete with others.

Join the eTapQuiz community today and start quizzing!

Key Features

  • Interactive quizzes
  • Various question types
  • Real-time feedback
  • Leaderboard
  • User-friendly interface
  • Customizable quizzes
  • Engaging learning experience

How to Use eTapQuiz

  1. Create an account or log in to your existing account.
  2. Explore the available quizzes or create your own.
  3. Join a quiz by entering the quiz code or participate in a public quiz.
  4. Answer the quiz questions within the given time limit.
  5. Receive instant feedback on your answers.
  6. Check the leaderboard to see how you rank against other participants.
  7. Review your quiz results and track your progress.
  8. Share your quiz scores with friends and challenge them to beat your score.
